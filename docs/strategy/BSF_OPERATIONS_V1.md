# BSF Node Operating Manual v1.0
## Bakersfield Food Recovery Protocol

### 1. Input Specifications
- **Source:** Verified Organic Debris (via YOLOv12 Audit).
- **Max Load:** 50kg per unit/day.
- **Moisture Target:** 60-70%.

### 2. The Conversion Cycle
- **Incubation:** 4 days from neonate to active feeder.
- **Active Processing:** 14 days of high-intensity consumption.
- **Harvest:** Pre-pupal stage for maximum protein density.

### 3. Economic Output
- **Frass:** High-grade organic fertilizer for Kern County agriculture.
- **Larvae:** Alternative protein for local aquaculture/poultry.
- **Methane Avoidance:** 100% reduction vs. anaerobic landfill decay.
