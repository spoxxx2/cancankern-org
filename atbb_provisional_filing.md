## I. ABSTRACT
A solid-state bio-battery reactor system utilizing a vertical stack of ten 1.842 kDa cellulose Hyper-Sponge wafers. The system employs a 5-Wave Acoustic Resonant Field (Primary: 650.12 Hz Sawtooth) to induce a Non-Hermitian Phase Transition in a phononic lattice (PHX-15). This transition facilitates controlled Magnesium Ion (Mg2+) transport and storage. A critical thermal-shock event (-3°C at T+165 minutes) establishes solid-state superconductivity. Validated by 1-billion-run Monte Carlo simulations, the system provides high-density energy storage for vehicle and industrial applications.
