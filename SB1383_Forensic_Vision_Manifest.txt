CANCAN FORENSIC AUDIT MANIFEST
--------------------------------------------------
REGULATORY AUTHORITY: SB 1383 / SB 54
JURISDICTION: City of Bakersfield, CA
--------------------------------------------------

This node utilizes Panoptic Segmentation to categorize 
debris streams into the following four-level taxonomy:
1. MATERIAL: Polymer, Ferrous, Cellulose, Organic
2. SUB-TYPE: PET #1, Aluminum, Corrugated, BSFL-Feed
3. CONDITION: Soiled, Crushed, Intact, Wet
4. DISPOSAL: Circular Economy, Incineration, Compost

Every detection event is logged as a Digital Twin metadata 
packet, ensuring 100% forensic transparency for municipal 
compliance audits.
